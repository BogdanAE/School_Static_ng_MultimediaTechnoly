3D Bravais Lattices

Bravais lattice is a lattice with translation symmetry which consists of equivalent nodes. There are 14 different Bravais lattices in 3D. Only three Bravais lattices with cubic symmetry (vrml) are shown here. Wigner-Zeitz cell consists of all points nearest to a node and has point symmetry of the corresponding Bravais lattice.
Simple Cubic lattice (vrml).
4x4x4 (vrml) and 6x6x6 (vrml) cells lattices.
Body Centered Cubic (BCC) unit cell (vrml)
2x2x2 (vrml) and 4x4x4 (vrml) cells lattices. α-Fe, Na, K, β-Ti have this structure. The Wigner-Zeitz cell for the BCC lattice is truncated octahedron (vrml).
Face Centered Cubic (FCC) unit cell (vrml).
2x2x2 (vrml) and 4x4x4 (vrml) lattices. Cu, Au, Ag, Al, Pt, Pd have this structure. The Wigner-Zeitz cell for the FCC lattice is rhombic dodecahedron (vrml).
